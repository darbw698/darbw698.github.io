On Beginnings

From Madness, Rack and Honey by Mary Ruefle

Webpage made by Luna Martha

Webpage

Luna's Stories as Network project on Mary Ruefle's reading 'On Beginnings' is a very put together website where the viewer is captured from the very beginning. Luna brings the viwer in with this sun rise that shows the title and author, the arrow that you click sends you to a page that is rows of text that scroll horizontal and move when you hover. There is a playfulness to it because there are highlighted parts that make up something else, if you need to see the full thing, This site shows that when you click the show full text tab.
